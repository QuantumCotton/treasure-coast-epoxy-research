## CHARGING - SIMPLE ANSWER

**No dedicated charger needed.** Both keyboards use:
- ✅ Standard USB-C cable (included)
- ✅ Any USB port on PC/laptop (works, slow charge)
- ✅ Any USB wall charger (works, fast charge)
- ✅ Any USB-C cable (upgrades work)
